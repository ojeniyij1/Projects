About Me

I'm a UX/UI developer and designer interested in designing and coding the best user experience possible. To me, UX/UI is more than just a career. It is the very thing that the current world functions on right now. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I started my IT journey working with Adobe Photoshop, creating mockups and graphic art. My passions include design, coding, crypto, fashion, music.

I love the brainstorming process and all the potential ideas that come to fruition. I also love the overall work process in building user interfaces and user design. In addition to programming, I have experience as a graphic designer. I work around the clock, but I do my best work from 6 am to 6 pm. For me personally, web development is more of an art. I see myself and my team as artists creating an ever-evolving interface for the world to function on.
