About Me

Developer & Designer

I'm a UX/UI developer and designer interested in designing and coding the best user experience possible. To me, UX/UI is more than just a career. It is the very thing that the current world functions on right now. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. In 2013, I gained experience in troubleshooting and coding for Velocious Delivery and started working with Adobe Photoshop, creating mockups and graphic art.
My passions include design, coding, crypto, fashion, music. I value hard work and creativity. I love the brainstorming process and all the potential ideas that come to fruition. I also have a very keen eye for details. I love the overall work process in building user interface and user design.
I learned 3 programming languages in 2 years. and am currently learning PHP. I am also a citizen of 3 countries. In addition to programming, I have experience as a graphic designer. I work around the clock, but I do my best work from 6 am to 6 pm. For me personally, web development is more of an art. I see myself and my team as artists creating an ever-evolving interface for the world to function on. I have certifications in MS Office and AWS. In 2010, I graduated from Strake Jesuit College Preporatory. I am also an Alumni of the Duke Talent program and NHS.
